Flink Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Flink, we collect and process the following information:

  • Organization email address
  • Profile information including name, registration number, phone number, date of birth, department, gender, blood group, languages, hobbies, and native place
  • Device information and FCM tokens for notifications
  • Location data (with your permission)
  • Usage data and interaction metrics
  • Profile images and media you choose to upload

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and maintain your Flink account
  • Provide core app functionality including messaging and profile discovery
  • Send notifications about app activities and updates
  • Generate analytics to improve our services
  • Maintain the security and integrity of our platform

3. Information Sharing

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • RoxyAPI Astrology Horoscope
  • DOTA API
  • Meme API
  • API Ninjas Quote Service

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your profile information may be visible to other verified members of your organization through the app's directory and discovery features.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Verification of organizational email addresses
  • Password hashing
  • Basic content filtering
  • User reporting and blocking capabilities

Please note that messages within the app are not end-to-end encrypted, and we may access messages for moderation purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and edit certain profile information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Report inappropriate content or behavior
  • Block other users

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Users will be notified of material changes to this policy.
